ABOUT ME

Venezuela was my home. I made the decision to come to the U.S. in 2015 due to the critical situation that has impacted my country, leaving behind my family, and the career I was pursuing in systems engineering. I started my new life without speaking a word of english but determined to succeed. In a few years, I was able to speak, write and read english and pursue goals that would have never been possible in my home country. One of my greatest discoveries was my passion for programming. I became eager to learn and took it into my own hands to teach myself, creating my first fully functional website for a local restaurant, utilizing html and css. From then on, I decided to pursue a career as a web developer and have been working endlessly towards expanding my skill set.
